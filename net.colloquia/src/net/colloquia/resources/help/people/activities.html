The People Folder

The Activities Tab

Clicking on a person's Activities Tab while in the People Folder displays a list of the Activities you and they have in common, the date it ended (or is due to end), and any grade they may have been given (but only if you are their tutor in that Activity).

You can navigate directly to that Activity by double clicking anywhere on the row that contains its name.

Comment: this allows a tutor to track all the courses and sub-activities on which they teach the student.
