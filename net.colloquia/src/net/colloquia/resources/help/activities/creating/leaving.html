Leaving an Activity

A participant can resign from an Activity at any time. All they need to do is highlight the Activity in their Activities Folder and click on Complete on the Colloquia Toolbar.

The Activity icon in your tree will also change to the icon. A resignation message will automatically be sent to the Activity owner, and your name will become greyed out on their version of the Activity. They can then send an Activity update to all participants, and cause your name on their trees to also become greyed out. They can now delete you, if they wish. You will no longer receive any Messages within this Activity.
