Sending My Details to Other People

If at any time you wish to send or update other Colloquia users with your personal details (name, photograph etc.) select the Send My Details option from the Communications menu. A wizard will guide you through the process. Note that the people to whom you can send your details shown in the wizard will depend on what is selected in the Tree:-

If an Activity (or a node in an Activity) is selected on the Tree only those people in that Activity will appear in the wizard. Otherwise, all people that share Activities with you are selected.
