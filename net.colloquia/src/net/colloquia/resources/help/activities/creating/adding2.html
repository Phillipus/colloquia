Adding a Sub-activity

Sub-activities allow you to structure your Activity by breaking it into sections. These may be time based (Week 1, Week 2, etc), or may be used to put People into groups. The process is exactly the same as adding an Activity.

Select the Activity with a single click and select New Activity from the Insert Menu. Now follow the guidelines offered by the "wizard", as you did before when adding the parent Activity. You will be given the option to have the sub-activity "inherit" the People from the parent Activity - if you wish this to happen, tick the box provided.

A new sub-Activity will appear within the selected Activity in your tree.

You can further populate the new sub-Activity at any time as described in the Activities Section.
