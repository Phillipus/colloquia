Replying to a Message from an individual

With the Message selected, click on Reply on the Conversations Toolbar. A new window will open, where all you have to do is:

  • Give your Message a title
  • Type your Message

Click on Send Now to send it immediately or click on to put it in your Outbox for sending later.

Your reply will be sent (or stored in your Outbox), and added to the list of Messages in the Conversation Index. If you wish to just save the Message as draft, click on Save . You can continue to edit the Message now or later by double clicking on it.
