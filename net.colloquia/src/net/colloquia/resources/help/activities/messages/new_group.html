Sending a New Message to the Group Conversation

If you click on New Message on the Conversations Toolbar, a new window will open, where all you have to do is:

  • Enter a new subject
  • Type your Message

Click on Send Now to send it immediately or click on to put it in your Outbox for sending later.

Your Message will be sent (or stored in your Outbox), and added to the list of Messages in the Conversation Index. If you wish to just save the Message as draft, click on Save As Draft . You can continue to edit the Message now or later by double-clicking on it.
