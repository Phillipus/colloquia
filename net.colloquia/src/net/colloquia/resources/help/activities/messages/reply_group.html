Replying to a Group Conversation Message

With the Message selected, click on Reply on the Conversations Toolbar. A new window will open which will allow you to edit the title if you wish, and then to type your Message.

Click on Send Now to send it immediately or click on to put it in your Outbox for sending later.

Your reply will be sent (or stored in the Message Centre Outbox), and added to the list of Messages in the Conversation Index. If you wish to just save the Message as draft, click on Save As Draft . You can continue to edit the Message now or later by double clicking on it. It will remain in italics until it is sent.
