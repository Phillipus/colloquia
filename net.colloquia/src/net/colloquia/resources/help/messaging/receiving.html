Receiving Messages in Colloquia

In most circumstances, you would set Colloquia up to automatically get Messages on loading the software (by ticking the Work on-line box in the Login Window), or you would get your Messages as follows:

Click on Get Mail on the Colloquia Toolbar or choose Get Mail from the Communications Menu.

You will be asked to enter your email password, and your Colloquia Messages will be downloaded. Finally you will be told how many Messages of each type you have received.

Wait a few minutes while your mail is downloaded.

Once all the Messages have been downloaded, you will see a Message enumerating any new messages received.

If you click on Message Centre in the dialogue box, you will be put in the Message Centre, from where you can get to any Message by double clicking on it (see the Messaging in Colloquia Section). If you click on OK, you can look the Activities tree to see where any unread Messages are located.
